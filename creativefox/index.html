3D Visualisation

Architectural drawings

Interior fit-outs

A project with Creative Fox provides excellence to the entire interior fit-out services including ceiling, partitions, flooring, wall finishes and so on. To make sure that the fit-out services provided by us are in tandem with the expectations of the clients is a priority for us. Clients come to us when they don't want a lot of hassle managing different sets of Interior design companies because they have Creative Fox as a one-stop destination to take care of all their fit-out needs in the Oman.

exhibition stands

Creative Fox’s design solutions are based on aesthetics. Aesthetic generate beauty, that is the secret ingredient which makes a design go further as a trade fair presentation, giving visitors a singular experience. Good design creates emotion for the brand or product. It takes only seconds for a company to be noticed, remembered or invisibly lost among competitors. Exhibition stand design and branding says a lot about the exhibitor. It is more powerful when it is uniquely and memorably presented.

visual Effects

We utilize a wide range of digital specials effects using particles to create rain, snow and debris using fluid simulation to create smoke, fire, or water. Our artists have the tools and training to create convincing digital effects as limitless as your imagination. Our special team of compositors are always raising the bar of photo-realism.

Reyan Ansari

Creative Lead

Hailing from the “City of Nawabs”, Lucknow, India, Reyan is the Creative Lead at Creative Fox. He has over a decade experience in CGI and with a background in visual effects, gaming, motion graphics and design, architecture visualization and 3D computer graphics soon became Reyan’s hobby and later his passion.

The Creation of something beautiful is his fundamental goal and motivation, combining a wide range of tools and expression methods. While off-work, Reyan enjoys learning about new discoveries in cosmology and playing computer games. Reyan is the Co-Founder of Creative Fox Visuals & Craft.
