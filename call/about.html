Who am I?

So, I’m Meredith McDonough- Intuitive Wise Woman, Healer, and Guide. As Intuitive Wise Woman I only deliver the Messages of Healing only to those who are open, ready, and willing to receive those Messages.

As Healer my role isn’t to heal you. Instead, my role is to create a space together where we go deep and continuously cultivate that unique healing space where you have “lightbulb” and “aha” moments and and as a result move toward self-healing.

As Guide to guide you through the journey and together we create the space to best achieve those healing outcomes you desire. I’m not doing the hike on your behalf or even carrying the pack.

What is The Call Within?

On the surface appears as a place where people arrive, get healing, get better and live their lives again in a much more at peace and content inner place. Beneath the surface, The Call Within is where the Unseen magical processes of self-healing meets everyday reality.

As a result, many of my clients have not only made changes in their life they’ve made waves! As a result, they lead much more authentically aligned life to who they really are.
