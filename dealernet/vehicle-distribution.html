INCREASE SPEED TO MARKET WITH IOL

Are you ready to take your dealership to the next level? Schedule an array of lot services right here. Our top-notch staff can get your photos, videos, labels, and buyers guides looking stellar in almost no time!

OUR SERVICES INCLUDE

  • Photography and Data Collection
  • Live Video Walk Around
  • Custom Labels and Buyers Guides
  • Craigslist Postings
  • Image Overlays
  • Photo Enhancement

Save yourself some time by scheduling any of these lot services right here on our site. Click the service you want below and fill out the contact form. After you submit the form, we’ll be in touch to work out the details. It doesn’t get much easier than that!

MULTIPLE SOURCES

IOL acquires data from just about any source, including: Reynolds and Reynolds certified, DealerTrack certified, DMS polling (ADP, UCS, etc.), Third party data feeds, Dealer spread sheets, and many more. Update different fields from different sources!

VIN DATA ENHANCEMENT

Premier VIN data enhancement with leading VIN data sets to mirror OEM data (ie: Chrome)

IMAGES & REPORTS

Standard stock images, Photos uploading, Image overlays, Image Enhancement, Data conversions, Customized dealer comments, Reports (Aged inventory, missing photos, etc.)

WINDOW STICKERS / BUYER'S GUIDES

Customizable layouts for both window stickers & buyer’s guides for labeling vehicles. Custom graphics and QR codes. DNS can also provide the sticker labels for ease and convenience. Create custom Addendums that reflect pricing on new cars.

PRICING MATRIX

Dealers can create fields-based pricing rules in IOL. Price adjustments can be determined by mileage or days in stock for pre-owned inventory. Rebates and incentives can be applied to new or used vehicles down to a unique model code or trim level.

PRICE ANALYSIS

Evaluates pricing for a particular vehicle, comparing it to similar vehicles on a local or national basis. Avoid vehicle birthdays by effectively pricing vehicles based on in-market data.

CARFAX / AUTOCHECK

With a CARFAX or Autocheck account you can purchase and or view the vehicle reports within IOL.

GM CERTIFIED

Allows GM dealers to enter certification inspection checklist number in IOL instead of the GM Certified backend system.

AUCTIONS

Post vehicles to auctions from IOL.
