WELCOME TO DEALERNET

Before we get ahead of ourselves, we want to thank you for your interest in DealerNet. We hope our digital impression has been informative and intriguing.

DealerNet was established in 2000 when the internet boom hit the automotive industry. We have grown substantially over the years and have several top performing dealerships as our customers. Our immediate goal is to give you an interactive tour of our services and solutions, as well as allow you to conveniently shop for print, promotional, apparel, and dealership supplies in our online store.

— Dustin Jansson, President of DealerNet

CORE VALUES

Profit in business comes from repeat customers, customers that boast about your product or service, and that bring friends with them. We built DealerNet on repeat and referred customers and will continue to validate our value through the satisfaction and success of our customers.

  • Educated and Knowledgeable on Dealer Operations
  • Integrated with industry leading technologies
  • Competitive Pricing and “One Stop Shop”
  • Highly recommended by the advertising vendors to fuel lead performance.
  • Flexible and Creative to adapt to YOUR dealership needs.

OUR ADVANTAGES

How much of your inventory is generating leads?

Inventory without photos does not generate leads. Take the Pepsi Challenge. What are your photo percentages? Do the math if you were at 90% pictured, how many more leads would you get?

What are dealership best practices?

We audit your process to see how you can be more successful with the speed to market process. Do your vehicles sometimes take a book hit before they make it out of recon? Do your photos and live video put your best foot forward or is it just good enough?

Are you evolving with the times or playing catch up?

Every dealer regardless of size advertises their inventory online. In the modern day we now have online-only dealers that don’t even have brick and mortar in the market and have an high quality picture and video presentation with their vehicles online advertising to compete with.
