We Offer

The vision of Esbjerg Survey Ltd. is to act as an independent survey company safeguarding the interests of our clients with due regard to a high grade of integrity.

  • Our main activities are to carry out:
  • Warranty Surveys on Heavy Cargo Shipments
  • Issuing Guidelines for Safe Stowage
  • Lashing and Securing
  • Risk Assessments for open Storage
  • Hull and Machinery Survey
  • P & I Condition Survey, Pre-Purchase Survey
  • Bunker and ON - OFF Hire Survey
  • Cargo Survey
  • IMCA CMID Inspection

Among our major clients are the windmill producers:

  • -Vestas Wind Systems
  • -MHI Vestas Offshore Wind
  • -Siemens Wind Power

and other clients:

  • -A2SEA
  • -Swire Blue Ocean
