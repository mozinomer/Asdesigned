Welcome

Esbjerg Survey Ltd. is an independent survey company in Denmark.
Esbjerg Survey Ltd. was founded in 1975 by Poul Petersen, Master Mariner and Master of Law, LL.M.
Over the years Esbjerg Survey Ltd. has established a postion as one of the leading independent survey company in Denmark with a staff with a broad range of experience.
The headquarter is located at Esbjerg and with branch offices at Lianyungang and Shanghai, China. Our main activities are in Europe and in the Far East but we offer our services all over the world.
