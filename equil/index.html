About Us

The Equipment Butler

The Equipment Butler was founded in 2019. Our sales team has a combined experience of over 35 years in the food processing industry.

We work diligently to provide you with high quality equipment and parts. In particular, the food processing, beverage and packaging industries. That is to say, we are committed to selling quality equipment at an exceptional value. In addition, providing all of our customers with first class service. We work with companies worldwide, giving us access to an extensive number of machines and experts.

Above all, we know the value of building true relationships with our customers and we hope to gain your trust. Thank you for stopping by. We look forward to hearing from you.
